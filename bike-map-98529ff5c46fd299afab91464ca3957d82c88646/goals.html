Cyclist Safety Kingston

Goals

Goal Introduction:

The goals for our team is to become a publicly available service to which the residents of Kingston can access with a simple Google search. The service that we anticipate providing is one that allows for cyclists to calculate new safer cycling routes as well as rate routes they've previously driven. Although the project is small scale for the moment, the team expects to become much more main stream and available for people around the world.

Future endeavors:

As stated previously Cycling Safety Kingston expects to grow within the next year and hopefully become a main stream service. Where users will be able to use Cycling Safety Kingston in different cities outside of Kingston. The team knows this is a long ways away, however we fully expect to achieve this level of success.
